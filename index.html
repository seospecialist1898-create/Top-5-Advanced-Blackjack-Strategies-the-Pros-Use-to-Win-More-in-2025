3. Use the 1-3-2-6 Betting System for Controlled Growth

Smart Bankroll Progression

This positive progression system helps you capitalize on winning streaks while limiting losses. Here’s how it works:

  1. Bet 1 unit
  2. If you win → Bet 3 units
  3. If you win → Bet 2 units
  4. If you win → Bet 6 units
  5. Win or lose → Restart at 1 unit

Example: Start with ₹100. Win four in a row = ₹1,200 profit. Lose at any point? You only risk your original bet.

4. Exploit Composition-Dependent Strategy (CDS)

Next-Level Precision

Basic strategy assumes average deck composition. CDS adjusts decisions based on exact cards in your hand.

Example: You have 16 (10+6) vs. dealer 10.

Basic strategy says Hit. But if the 6 is the only low card and many 10s are out, Stand may be better.

Pros use CDS in high-stakes Blackjack 21 games. Practice with simulators or apps that show card history.
